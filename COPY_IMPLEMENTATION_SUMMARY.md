### 1. ✅ HERO SECTION (Hero.tsx)

#### Changes:
- **Tagline (small label)**
  - OLD: "Your friendly Bitcoin uncle"
  - NEW: "FOR ANYONE WHO BOUGHT BITCOIN AND ISN'T SURE WHAT HAPPENS NEXT"
  - **Impact**: More specific to target audience, immediate relevance

  - OLD: "We read the charts and the whitepapers so you don't have to. The 5-minute newsletter that separates the signal from the noise."
  - NEW: "You bought some. Now what? Every day, we tell you exactly what's happening — in plain English, no charts, no jargon."

- **CTA Button**
  - OLD: "Start Reading"

  - **Impact**: More specific action, shows what they'll get

- **CTA Subtext**
  - OLD: "Join 45,000+ subscribers."
  - NEW: "No Spam. No Noise. Unsubscribe Any Time."
  - **Impact**: Removes social proof pressure, focuses on trust

- **Company Logo Social Proof**
  - OLD: "Read by everyday investors at [companies]"
  - NEW: "Read by 1,000+ people who just wanted to understand what they bought."
  - **Impact**: More relatable, specific to reader intent, not company prestige

### 4. ✅ VALUES SECTION (AboutPage.tsx)

#### Changes (All 4 Values updated):
- **Signal Over Noise**
  - OLD: "We filter out 99% of the news to bring you the 1% that actually matters for your understanding."
  - NEW: "Hundreds of Bitcoin stories happen every day. Most of them don't matter to you. We read everything and tell you the one thing that does."

- **Trust First → Journalists, Not Influencers**
  - OLD: "We are journalists, not influencers. We verify sources and don't accept paid shills."
  - NEW: "We don't hold Bitcoin. We don't have affiliate deals. We verify sources and we don't get paid to say nice things about products."
  - **Impact**: Much more specific about what we DON'T do, builds confidence

- **Global View → The World, Not Just The Charts**
  - OLD: "Bitcoin is a global phenomenon. We cover stories from Lagos to London, not just Silicon Valley."
  - NEW: "Bitcoin is happening in El Salvador, in Lagos, in Brussels, in a garage in Texas. We cover all of it — not just the price."
  - **Impact**: More vivid examples, specific locations show research

- **Respect Time → We Respect Your Time**
  - OLD: "We know you're busy. We get to the point immediately. No fluff, no filler."
  - NEW: "You're busy. Five minutes. One story. We get to the point and we stop."
  - **Impact**: More punchy, creates a clear contract with reader
